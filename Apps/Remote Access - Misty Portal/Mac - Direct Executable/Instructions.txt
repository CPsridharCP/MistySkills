ONLY THE FIRST TIME
The first time you open the app, there may be an unverfied developer error.
To allow the app to run follow thesee two steps.
1. Choose Apple menu > System Preferences, click Security & Privacy, then click General.
2. You can grant an exception for a blocked app by clicking the “Open Anyway” button in the General pane. This button is available for about an hour after you try to open the app.
